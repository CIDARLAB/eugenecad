What is Eugene?


Eugene is an ecosystem of human- and machine-readable languages developed to speed up and innovate the design of novel biological DNA Sequences, Parts, Devices, and Systems. The overarching goal is to create a clear and standardized language for Synthetic Biology.

Eugene's key facilities for the synthetic biology domain include:
  • Specify synthetic biological components at various levels of abstraction
  • Define constraints and rules on the components' compositions
  • Manage the control-flow of design synthesis
  • Utlize Eugene's Apps to manage your Part and Design Library or to generate novel biological devices automatically
  • Prototype and invoke user-required and reusable functions
